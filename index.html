About Telam Partners

Precision counsel
for complex markets.

Telam Partners is a senior-level advisory firm headquartered in Barcelona. We work with international funds, multinational corporations, and industrial ventures seeking strategic guidance across the Spanish market and beyond.

Our practice is built on deep market knowledge, long-standing institutional relationships, and a rigorous approach to capital deployment and strategic positioning. We bring senior expertise — not junior teams — to every engagement.

We have advised on and supported more than $12 billion USD in projects spanning infrastructure, real estate, financial services, and industrial sectors.
